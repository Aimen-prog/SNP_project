Help page

Search buttons detail:


Phenotype Search button:
Search for a phenotype(Trait/Disease) and get related informations in a datatable. You can also get informations about related SNP by clicking on the Rsid of interest.
Phenotype List button:
See all available traits and diseases and get informations about a phenotype by clicking on its name, you can also get informations about related SNP by clicking on the Rsid of interest.
SNP Search buttons:
Search for an SNP of interest and find its related informations, you can also get informations about related phenotype by clicking on the name of the phenotype.
You can search by:
→ The Rsid: Search only by entering the Rsid, suggestions are provided with a scroll menu.
Note: Enter only the number ( without "rs" at the beginning).
→ The region of interest: Search an SNP by scanning a specific chromosome part and/or by selecting the chromosome number for more precision.
Note: This process could take some time depending on selected chromosome range.

Headers detail:


Pubmed id: PubMed identification number.
Date: Publication date (online (epub) date if available).
Journal: Abbreviated journal name.
Study: Title of paper.
Disease/Trait: Disease or trait examined in study.
Chromosome number: Chromosome number associated with rs number.
Chromosome position: Chromosomal position associated with rs number.
Rsid: current rs number (can differ from strongest SNP if merged).
Pvalue: Reported p-value for strongest SNP risk allele (linked to dbGaP Association Browser).
Note: p-values are rounded to 1 significant digit (for example, a published p-value of 4.8 x 10-7 is rounded to 5 x 10-7).
